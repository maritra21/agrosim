The AgroConnect Story

Bridging the gap between Kumira and GEC Circle

Abdul Karim wakes before Fajr every morning to tend to his crops near Kumira. His vegetables are the freshest in the area, but he struggles with inconsistent earnings and unreliable middlemen.

Just 25 kilometers away in GEC Circle, Shahed Alam runs a popular cafe and desperately wants a steady source of high-quality, local produce.

AgroConnect bridges this gap, creating a direct connection that benefits everyone.
